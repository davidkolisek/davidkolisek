About me

About me

A few words...

I'm a frontend developer focused on turning designs into fast, maintainable websites and web applications — mostly with Vue.js and Nuxt, plus React when the project calls for it.

With over 6 years of professional web development experience, I aim for high Lighthouse scores and clean, accessible markup. I consider a project done when it performs well, works across browsers and devices, and is easy to hand over.

Throughout the project's lifecycle, I maintain active communication with the client. This collaborative approach is a cornerstone of my process, designed to streamline project development and ensure timely delivery of the final product.

I am adaptable when it comes to teamwork, comfortable both in collaborative environments and as an independent contributor. I have extensive experience working with the Scrum methodology, but I'm equally at ease with direct client interaction.
